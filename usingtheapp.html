How the Kløpper app works

Step 1:
Download the app

For more information please see our step-by-step instructions on how to download the app below.

Step 2:
Sign up for an account

After downloading the app, you will be guided through step-by-step instructions on how to make a profile.

Step 3:
Choose between your favourite games

Let the fun begin! Choose between a myriad of your favourite games and activities, make new friends, and have fun!

Step 4:
Stay up to date

Stay up do date with our in-app event calendar. Here you can sign up for events and set reminders, so that you never have to miss a day.

Step 5:
Stay in touch with friends

Add friends to your friend list in order to stay in touch. Gather the gang for regular hang-outs!

Step 6:
Earn points and prizes

Use your bingo-mojo to build an impressive prize collection. Show it off to your friends!

Downloading the Kløpper app

Step 1.

Visit the App store on your smartphone or tablet (links below)

Step 2.

Find us by entering "Kløpper" in the search field.

Step 3.

Press Download and Open.

If you have an Apple product, this is your app store: Apple app store
