Here's how the app works

1. Download the app

For more information on how to download the app please visit our 'for users'-page.

2. Sign up for an account

After downloading the app, you will be guided through step-by-step instructions on how to make a profile.

3. Choose between your favourite games!

Let the fun begin! Choose between a myriad of your favourite games, make new friends, and have fun!

4. Stay up to date

Stay up do date with our in-app event calendar. Here you can sign up for events and set reminders, so that you never have to miss a day.

5.Stay in touch with friends

Add friends to your friend list in order to stay in touch. Gather the gang for regular hang-outs!

6.Earn points and prizes

Use your bingo-mojo to build an impressive prize collection. Show it off to your friends!
