For Volunteers

Help us reach even more people by arranging events of your own!

Our inspiration


We will be responsible for a range of acitivities hosted by our team. But following the expression “the more the merrier” , we believe that it could do so much more if we let organizations and engaged citizens participate too! We’re therefore building a organisator part of our app for those who want to organize online events. This is still under construction, but hopefully you will be able to host events on our app. If you want to know when this is live, send us an email at @ and we will let you know when it's launched!
