Bingo nights, quizzes, knitting clubs, card games, and more..

all from the comfort of your home.

Who says seniors can't have fun?

Due to COVID-19, health reasons, slippery pavements and other hinderances, many a bingo night has been cancelled. Kløpper is the app which lets you take control and finally say: cancelled bingo nights no more!

Kløpper offers a digital social meeting space for people aged 60+. Here you can join scheduled activities such as workouts, bingo, knitting, and more, all the while talking to and seeing each other face to face over video chat. With Kløpper, there is no limitation that can stop you from enjoying the social activities that you once used to. Just like you could back in the old days.

"Kløpper"; [Norwegian, noun] a person who is skilled. For example: in card games, bingo, or even just being friendly!

Bring out your inner Kløpper and join us today in the Kløpper app!
