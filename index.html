Who says seniors can't have fun?

Are you more than 60 years old? Do you love hanging out with friends? Here at Kløpper we invite you to join our scheduled online social events. We offer activities such as workouts, bingo, knitting, and more!

Here you can meet lots of new friends and chat with each other over video, face to face, while having tons of fun. With Kløpper, there is no physical limitation that can stop you from enjoying just as many social activities as you want to.

We look forward to seeing you there!

This is what they say about us:

Harrison, 78

Due to bad health I have to stay at home a lot. Even going for a walk or the store can be too much some days.

This app has helped me meet friends without even leaving the house. Kløpper has given me a chance to connect that I wouldn't have had otherwise.

Dolly, 74

I started using this app to find someone to do crafts with. I knit and crochet a lot, but it can get kind of lonely to just knit in silence.

Through Kløpper I met some lovely ladies. Now we all videochat together while knitting for our grandkids!

Arnold, 73

After my arthritis got worse, it has been more difficult getting out of the house to play cards with my friends.

After we downloaded Kløpper, it has been much easier to keep in touch just like we used to.
